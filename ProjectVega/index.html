What Vega is, and what it’s trying to become.

Project Summary

Hospital Starship Vega is a long-form universe presented as a visual book with episodic chapters, character dossiers, ship schematics, and curated “case files.” The tone is compassionate but unsentimental: the ship saves lives, and sometimes it has to negotiate with the universe to do it.

This page is designed to grow with you. Background art, concept renders, and media embeds can be dropped in later without restructuring anything.
