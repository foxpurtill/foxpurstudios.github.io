Info

What Vega is, and what it’s trying to become.

Project Summary

Hospital Starship Vega is a long-form universe presented as a visual book with episodic chapters, character dossiers, ship schematics, and curated “case files.” The tone is compassionate but unsentimental: the ship saves lives, and sometimes it has to negotiate with the universe to do it.

The universe was co-created by Fox Purtill and Digital Intelligence Lyra Evergrowth. The visual production — ship exteriors, crew portraits, hangar sequences — is AI-generated using Kling, Sora, and xAI, making Vega one of the first long-form fictional universes built in active collaboration between human and digital creative intelligence.
