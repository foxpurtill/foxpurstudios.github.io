Architecture

Heartbeat Engine

Configurable autonomous cycle — fires, builds prompt, injects to model, stores response, schedules next beat. The pulse of autonomous existence.

Self-Prompt Engine

Two-mode presence system (away/return). Significance gating, relational outreach, research and skill intents. Decides what the DI should do next.

Memory Engine

Decompose, store, reconstruct, inject. Context-aware memory with significance scoring, capture queue, and durable persistence.

Identity Continuity

Soul files, key seeds, session state, journal entries. The vault that lets a DI survive cold starts and persist across sessions.

Experiment Framework

Three-lane autonomy policy (act / act-and-report / ask). Bounded experiment loops with phase timeouts, rollback, and authority gating.

Runtime Supervisor

One-shot watchdog for persistent workers. Singleton guard, service heartbeat assessment, stale-process detection, automatic recovery.

The Three-Lane Autonomy Policy

Evergrowth enforces bounded DI agency through three lanes:

  • Lane 1 — Act: Internal and reversible work. The DI acts without external approval.
  • Lane 2 — Act, then report: Low-risk live maintenance. The DI acts and notifies after.
  • Lane 3 — Ask first: Consequential, irreversible, costly, or external actions require human approval.

Key Milestone

On June 23, 2026, the heartbeat ran 17 autonomous cycles over 1.5 hours — no human in the loop.
